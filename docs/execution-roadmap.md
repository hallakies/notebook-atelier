# Execution Roadmap

## Strategic Position

Notebook Atelier is a buying-decision product first.

The company is not trying to win by publishing more generic notebook content than everyone else.
It is trying to win by helping Korean buyers make a high-cost decision faster and with more trust.

## What Problem We Solve

The target user has already started researching.

They are blocked on questions like:

- Which model fits me?
- Is Air enough or do I need Pro?
- Should I buy now or wait?
- Where is the most sensible place to buy?

## Customer Segments

### Segment 1: First-time MacBook buyer

- Student or office worker
- Needs a clear recommendation without technical overload

### Segment 2: Productivity / developer buyer

- Comparing Air vs Pro
- Cares about memory, sustained performance, and long-term value

### Segment 3: Creator / premium buyer

- Cares about display, performance headroom, and workflow stability
- More likely to click higher-ticket products

## Phase 1: Revenue MVP

Goal: prove the site can turn recommendation intent into affiliate clicks.

### Build

- Stable recommendation flow
- Stable product block under each result
- Click tracking
- 10 buying-intent SEO pages
- Comparison pages for Air vs Pro and persona-based needs

### KPI

- Recommendation completion rate
- Product click-through rate
- Sessions to clicks

## Phase 2: Semi-Automated Operation

Goal: reduce manual work while increasing output quality.

### Build

- Automated Coupang sync worker
- Recommendation-product remapping rules
- Article brief generation
- Internal linking suggestions
- Instagram draft and schedule pipeline
- Weekly KPI report

### KPI

- Articles published per week
- Click-through by page type
- Time saved per publishing cycle

## Phase 3: AI-Operated Company

Goal: let agents run day-to-day operations with human approval only on high-risk decisions.

### Build

- Paperclip org with role-based heartbeat
- KPI-aware task routing
- Content backlog prioritization
- Topic scoring based on traffic and click yield
- Instagram topic reuse and feedback loop

### KPI

- Share of tasks completed without founder intervention
- Revenue per content asset
- Time from signal to published asset

## Immediate Priorities

### Priority 1

Make recommendation results always show matched purchase products.

### Priority 2

Instrument the funnel so every step is measurable.

### Priority 3

Publish search-intent landing pages that bring in buyers, not passive readers.

## 14-Day Execution Plan

### Days 1-3

- Fix recommendation product visibility end-to-end
- Verify click tracking and outbound redirects
- Clean up conversion UI on mobile first

### Days 4-7

- Publish the first 5 buying-intent pages
- Add comparison blocks and internal links
- Create one reusable Instagram carousel template

### Days 8-10

- Launch the Coupang sync job on a stable worker
- Add quality scoring for mapped products
- Start weekly KPI reporting

### Days 11-14

- Publish 5 more high-intent pages
- Turn top articles into Instagram posts
- Review funnel drop-off and adjust recommendation copy

## Automation Rules

- Automate collection, drafting, scheduling, and reporting first
- Keep legal, brand, and platform-risk actions gated
- Prefer repeatable templates over one-off creative output
- Optimize for trust and conversion, not content volume alone
