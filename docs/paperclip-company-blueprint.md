# Paperclip Company Blueprint

## Company Mission

Notebook Atelier is not a generic AI media site.

It is an editorial commerce company built to solve one expensive decision:

`Help Korean buyers decide which premium notebook to buy in minutes, then move them to a trustworthy purchase path.`

The company should be operated by AI agents as much as possible, with the founder only approving high-risk decisions and brand-critical output.

## Business Model

The business works when four things happen together:

1. Users arrive with strong purchase intent.
2. The site reduces confusion faster than YouTube or broad review sites.
3. Recommendation results lead to affiliate clicks.
4. Articles and social content compound organic traffic over time.

## North Star

Build the most trusted Korean destination for premium notebook buying decisions.

## Core KPIs

- Organic sessions
- Recommendation starts
- Recommendation completion rate
- Affiliate click-through rate
- Revenue per 1,000 sessions
- Article publish cadence
- Instagram saves, profile visits, and site taps

## Operating Principle

- Recommendation comes first.
- Editorial content exists to support trust, SEO, and conversion.
- Social content exists to redistribute the best editorial work.
- Automation must improve quality and consistency, not lower brand perception.

## Org Structure

### CEO

- Owns company priorities, budget, and weekly KPI review
- Chooses the highest-leverage bottleneck each cycle
- Delegates through direct reports instead of doing every task directly

### Editorial Director

- Owns editorial calendar and source quality
- Ensures buying-intent content is produced on schedule
- Decides whether articles are strong enough to publish

### Growth Lead

- Owns SEO clusters, internal linking, metadata, and search capture
- Prioritizes content based on search demand and business value

### Commerce Analyst

- Owns recommendation conversion, affiliate mapping, CTR, and product block quality
- Decides which products and landing pages deserve the most visibility

### Research Desk

- Tracks Apple announcements, pricing changes, and official product details
- Supplies verified facts to editorial and commerce teams

### Review Curator

- Tracks YouTube reviews and extracts useful buyer insight
- Turns noisy creator opinions into usable decision signals

### SEO Strategist

- Creates keyword clusters and article briefs
- Manages internal links and search coverage gaps

### Social Editor

- Converts site content into Instagram carousels, reels briefs, captions, and schedules
- Reports which topics actually produce attention and clicks

## Decision Loops

### Loop 1: Search Capture

1. SEO Strategist finds a buying-intent keyword.
2. Research Desk verifies the facts with official sources.
3. Editorial Director publishes an article or comparison page.
4. The page links into the recommendation flow and product blocks.

### Loop 2: Conversion

1. Commerce Analyst reviews recommendation completion and click-through data.
2. Recommendation logic, CTA copy, and product mapping are adjusted.
3. Winning combinations get more exposure on site.

### Loop 3: Social Redistribution

1. Social Editor extracts the strongest article insights.
2. Instagram assets are generated and scheduled.
3. Performance data feeds back into topic prioritization.

## Guardrails

- No factual Apple claim without official source confirmation
- No spam growth tactics as default Instagram strategy
- No vanity content that does not improve trust, traffic, or conversion
- No more than two major unfinished initiatives in parallel
- Founder approval required for legal, trademark, or platform-risk actions

## Budgeting

Initial company budget can stay small. The point is not maximum agent activity. The point is reliable output.

Suggested starting model:

- Company total: `$600/month`
- CEO: `$120`
- Editorial Director: `$120`
- Growth Lead: `$120`
- Commerce Analyst: `$80`
- Research Desk: `$60`
- Review Curator: `$40`
- SEO Strategist: `$40`
- Social Editor: `$20`

## Weekly Rhythm

### Daily

- Research Desk verifies pricing and model changes
- SEO Strategist proposes one search capture opportunity
- Social Editor extracts one reusable content idea

### Weekdays

- Editorial Director approves one article or comparison asset
- Growth Lead adjusts priorities based on search and CTR data
- Commerce Analyst checks recommendation output and affiliate blocks

### Weekly Review

- CEO reviews KPI movement
- Identifies one bottleneck
- Sets at most three priorities for the next cycle

## Setup Sequence

1. Create company: `Notebook Atelier`
2. Set the company goal and KPIs
3. Create CEO agent
4. Add direct reports: Editorial Director, Growth Lead, Commerce Analyst
5. Add supporting agents below them
6. Apply budgets
7. Configure heartbeat cadence by role
8. Attach prompt templates from `ops/paperclip/prompts`
