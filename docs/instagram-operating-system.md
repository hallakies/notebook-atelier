# Instagram Operating System

## Objective

Instagram is not the business by itself.

Its job is to do three things:

1. Turn site insight into attention
2. Bring qualified users to the profile and site
3. Validate which notebook topics deserve more editorial investment

## Automation Boundary

Default automation should stop at:

- content planning
- carousel structure generation
- caption draft generation
- scheduling
- performance reporting

Avoid default automation for:

- mass follow / unfollow
- spam comments
- aggressive outbound DM

That behavior increases platform risk and weakens the brand.

## Content Pillars

### Buying Decision Carousel

- Which MacBook should I buy?
- Air vs Pro in one minute
- Best MacBook for students
- Best MacBook for developers

### Timing and Pricing Brief

- Is now a good time to buy?
- Current price logic
- Which model is worth paying extra for?

### Premium Notebook Edit

- Best premium notebook for mobility
- Best notebook for design work
- Best notebook for office and multitasking

### Reel Briefs

- 20-second recommendation summary
- Fast buyer mistakes to avoid
- One sharp opinion from a larger article

## Weekly Publishing Rhythm

- Monday: buying-decision carousel
- Wednesday: price or timing brief
- Friday: comparison carousel
- Weekend: reel brief or story sequence

## Content Workflow

1. Editorial content is published on site.
2. Social Editor extracts 6 to 10 high-signal points.
3. Those points are converted into slide structure and caption draft.
4. Assets are rendered into publishing format.
5. Posts are scheduled.
6. Results are reviewed at 24 hours, 72 hours, and 7 days.

## Caption Rules

- The first line must open a decision or tension.
- Avoid generic hype language.
- Use 3 to 5 focused hashtags, not large random sets.
- End with one action only: visit site, compare models, or save post.

## Success Metrics

- Save rate
- Profile visit rate
- Link click-through
- Reel watch-through
- Topic-level contribution to site traffic and clicks

## Stack Direction

- Source of truth: site articles and recommendation data
- Planning and scripts: Paperclip Social Editor
- Asset generation: template-based image or design pipeline
- Scheduling: Meta Graph API or an approved scheduler
- Analytics: Instagram Insights + GA4 + PostHog

## Brand Rules

- Premium, restrained tone
- No obvious AI wording
- No fake authority
- No Apple impersonation
- Useful, calm, decisive language
